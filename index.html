The Leadership Team

Senior professionals with decades of experience spanning investments, technology, and education.

Yoonjai Shin CFA, CMT

Founder & CEO

Yoonjai leads Baritone's investment research and strategy development. With more than two decades of experience, he designs innovative research frameworks and financial technology platforms, integrating data science with time-tested investment principles. Previously, he was a VP and Portfolio Manager at CI Global Asset Management, where he co-managed more than $35 billion in multi-asset mandates, helping to drive industry recognition and the modernization of the multi-asset platform. He is a Registered Portfolio Manager; registration is held through a Canadian registered dealer.

Ted Brown CFP, CIM

Managing Partner

Ted brings more than 20 years of experience in portfolio management and financial planning, with deep expertise in portfolio construction, client risk management, and the implementation of advanced investment frameworks within advisory practices. His diverse career spans Edward Jones, RBC Dominion Securities, and the Bank of Montreal, with international teaching experience across Beijing, Taipei, and London — shaping a global perspective on markets and disciplined execution. He is an Associate Portfolio Manager; registration is held through a Canadian registered dealer.
